Sambar Masala

Ingredients- Procedure how to make Sambar- 1. Take 50 gms of tur daal & wash it. 2. Pour 400 ml water in the cooker. Add a pinch of salt, turmeric and asetofocida powder to it. 3. Add a spoon of oil & pressure cook daal. Add chopped & boiled vegetables (250gms) fresh grated coconut & 2 tsp of Ruchira Sambar Masala to the cooked daal. 4. Heat 2 spoon of oil in a pan. Add mustard cumin seeds, red dry chilli, curry leaves to the hot oil & add in the daal. 5. Garnish with coriander leaves & serve hot.

Chinese Masala

Take a 200gms of plain noodles packet and add it to the boiling water and cook the noodles. 3. Once the noodles are cooked drain the water. 4. In a pan add oil and let it heat. Add chopped garlic, dry red chilli, chopped vegetables of your choice cook it for a while. 5. To the above mixture add the boiled noodles, 1 table spoon Ruchira Chinese masala and cook it further. 6. Once mixed properly add soya sauce, chilli sauce, tomato sauce and vinegar according to your taste. 7. Sizzling and yummy Chinese is ready for serving.
